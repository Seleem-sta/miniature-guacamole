Brand Story

Our Origin

OL'Navae draws its name from the ancient olive groves that line the Mediterranean coast and the navy blue waters that have carried traders, dreamers, and artisans for millennia. We are a contemporary clothing brand rooted in the belief that true elegance comes from simplicity and respect for the natural world.

"Our collections are designed for the modern individual who moves through life with grace and intention—someone who appreciates quality over quantity."

Each piece is thoughtfully crafted using sustainable materials and ethical production methods, honoring both the artisans who create them and the environment that inspires them. Our designs transcend seasons and trends, created to be cherished for years to come.

Logo Don'ts

Don't
  • Stretch or distort the logo
  • Change the logo colors
  • Add effects (shadows, gradients)
  • Place on busy backgrounds
  • Rotate or tilt the logo
Do
  • Maintain aspect ratio
  • Use approved color variations
  • Keep logo clean and flat
  • Ensure sufficient contrast
  • Use logo horizontally

Brand Values

Sustainability

We use organic, recycled, and responsibly sourced materials in every garment we create. From fabric selection to packaging, we minimize our environmental footprint at every step.

Timeless Design

Our pieces transcend seasons and trends, designed to be cherished for years to come. We believe in creating wardrobe staples that never go out of style.

Ethical Production

We partner with artisans and factories that uphold fair wages and safe working conditions. Every person in our supply chain is treated with dignity and respect.

Mediterranean Soul

Every design is infused with the relaxed elegance and natural beauty of coastal living. We capture the essence of Mediterranean lifestyle in every piece.

Application Guidelines

Digital Applications

Website
Cream background, Navy text, Sage accents. Clean layouts with generous whitespace.
Social Media
Natural photography, muted tones, consistent filters. Logo in corner or watermark.
Email
Simple layouts, clear hierarchy. Navy headers, cream background, minimal decoration.
Digital Ads
Product-focused imagery, minimal text. Logo always present, clear CTAs.

Print Applications

Business Cards
Cream or white stock, Navy logo. Minimal information, elegant typography.
Hang Tags
Recycled kraft paper, Navy or Terracotta print. Include care instructions.
Packaging
Recyclable materials, minimal branding. Tissue paper in Sage or Cream.
Lookbooks
Matte paper, generous imagery. Editorial layout with clean typography.

Photography Style

Element Guideline
Lighting Natural, soft, golden hour preferred. Avoid harsh artificial light.
Setting Coastal, Mediterranean-inspired locations. Olive groves, beaches, rustic architecture.
Color Grading Warm, muted tones. Enhanced greens and blues. Avoid oversaturation.
Models Natural, confident, relaxed poses. Minimal makeup, natural hair.
Composition Clean, uncluttered. Negative space encouraged. Product always in focus.
